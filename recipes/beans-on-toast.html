Beans on Toast

Description

This easy and delious meal is a british classic!

The steps to make a hearty and filling beans on toast will be detailed below!

Ingredients

  • Tin a baked beans.
  • Slices of bread
  • Butter (optional)
  • Cheese (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the beans in a saucepan.
  2. Toast the bread and butter it.
  3. Pour beans over the buttered toast.
  4. Top with cheese.
