Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is delicious and a favoured dish of cats and humans alike.

The steps to make a lasagne will be detailed below!

Ingredients

  • Pasta Sheets
  • Beef
  • Tinned Chopped Tomatos
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Cook the beef.
  2. Add the Tomatos and simmer.
  3. Stack sauce and pasta sheets.
  4. Top with cheese and bake in oven.
